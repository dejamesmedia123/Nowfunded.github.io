Challenge Rules

Phase 1 → Phase 2 → Funded → Scaled
Every challenge has two evaluation phases before funding. Phase 1 has a higher profit target than Phase 2 — clear them both, staying within the max drawdown, and we issue your live MT5 credentials.
Breaching the max drawdown during Phase 1 or Phase 2 means the challenge fee is lost. There's no reset and no refund — you're free to purchase a new challenge from the Marketplace at any time.
Breaching drawdown on a funded or scaled account marks it as blown. Depending on current promotions, a portion of your original fee may be refunded as points — this is shown on your dashboard if it applies to you.
No — every progression (passing a phase, issuing credentials, marking funded or scaled) is reviewed and actioned manually by our team. You'll get a Telegram notification the moment your status changes.
📉

Drawdown

Drawdown is measured at the account level, calculated end-of-day. There is no intraday or daily drawdown limit — only the overall maximum shown on your plan matters.
Your max drawdown percentage is shown on your plan card in the Marketplace and again on your Dashboard under your current account's linked plan details.

Points

You earn points when a trader you referred has their payment confirmed, and — depending on current promotions — when a funded or scaled account is blown, a portion of the lost fee can be refunded to you as points.
Points are redeemable in the Points Shop for discount codes on your next challenge purchase. They can't be withdrawn or used for anything else.
Open the Points & Referrals tab to see your current balance, your full points history, and the codes available to redeem.
🤝

Referrals

Every trader gets a unique referral code and shareable link. When someone you referred signs up and their first challenge payment is confirmed by our team, you're awarded points automatically.
Only once — and exactly when — your referred trader's payment is confirmed. Pending or rejected orders don't earn points.
Your referral code and copyable link live on the Points & Referrals page, along with a history of who you've referred and their status.
